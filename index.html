PRODUCT FOR VERTICALS

Our suite of products for your fin-tech need

GRAVITY

GROUP LOANS
  • 'Gravity’ is our flagship Origination System & CRM platform for group & individual loans where all field level activities happen through the Android app & all back-end activities including Underwriting & monitoring flows through the web based platform.

FOS

INDIVIDUAL LOANS
  • 'FoS’ is a Loan Origination, underwriting & CRM platform for SME loans, LAP, Housing, Affordable Housing, where multiple levels of credit decisions and Deviation matrix provides you ease of underwriting.

HONOR

COLLECTION
  • 'Honor’ is an independent collection management & Attendance Tracking platform which helps organisation to have seamless collection at field level. Monitoring & maker checker ensures high quality of transaction.

CO

CO-ORIGINATION
  • 'CO' is eSthenos co-origination platform which helps Banks & NBFCs to co-originate high-quality loans. It provides feature to onboard multiple partners with exclusive business rules for each partner. Platform can be integrated with multiple loan partners and thus enabling Banks / NBFC’s to manage lending across multiple partners on a single platform.

BANYAN

ANALYTICS
  • 'Banyan’ is our integrated Business Intelligence & Analytics platform to help Organisations in efficiently managing and analysing its critical business information to offer tailor made product & services to end customer.

LEADR

LEAD MANAGEMENT
  • 'LEADR' is a business lead generation & management platform which enables financial institutions to create Pipeline, increase Sales Productivity & Win Rate. Leads from all the channels at one platform.

THEIA

IMAGE PROCESSING
  • 'THEIA' helps user organization in image processing, face detection and tagging of explicit content. It helps organization to reduce back office cost by substantial margin. THEIA helps in verifying information in Image viz a viz text [bank passbook, KYC, customer images etc.] and improves the TAT.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Some of the FAQ from your side, related to our platform & product offering and we love to answer those

1. Do you have dedicated product for secured loans like LAP (Loan against property) ?

Our ‘fos’ platform is dedicated to all kind of secured loan, such as Housing, LAP, affordable housing etc. It’s also supports SME & small business loans which requires work flow-based underwriting in absence of proper credit history of probable borrower.

2. How strong is data security policy, for frontend & backend?

  • a. At rest data is encrypted with bank grade 256-bit AES encryption.
  • b. All services are ring fenced with defense in depth strategy.
  • c. Customer data is placed on private machines with no access to the internet. Only trusted machines within the eSthenos network can access the database.
  • d. Real time replication of database to prevent data loss along with configurable snapshot strategy.
  • e. Real time log monitoring and alerting for detecting suspicious activity.

3. Do you offer disbursement kit/CAM sheet in local languages?

We support most Indian languages on our platform.

4. Do you only have mobile application or web application also?

The platform comprises a Mobile, Web application where all the activities which supposed to be done at field or on the go happens on mobile and the activities which needs to be done at office or branch level can be done using web application.

5. It is integrated with any Bank for API based disbursement?

We offer API based disbursement directly to the customer account.

6. Is your platform integrated with eKYC platform?

Yes, we have integration with multiple eKYC providers

7. Does the platform support cross sale products?

Yes. Currently our partner organization offers 30+ cross sale products using ‘gravity’.

8. Do you integrate your platform with existing CBS/LMS?

We support API / bulk file based integration with CBS/LMS.

9. Do you have separate analytics platform?

‘Banyan’ is our analytics platform. Currently its available with our flagship products only and our existing users can have access to it after subscribing to ‘banyan’ platform separately.

10. Does your platform support bank BC Partnership Model?

Yes, it does support bank BC model and currently our platform is being used by some of the biggest BC Bank partners of the country for sourcing & collections management

Co-origination

Co-origination is a new system introduced by RBI in the wake of the liquidity crisis in non-banking finance to enhance credit flow to productive sectors.

The co-origination mechanism also gives the banking sector another avenue to meet their priority sector lending (PSL) requirements which include securitization, business correspondent model apart from direct lending.

eSthenos - a frontrunner in solving financial business automation complexity has launched its plug and play co-lending platform for banks and NBFCs where it gets integrated with loan originating partners system and other hand with lending organization.

It has already processed more than INR 200 Crs for its existing partners through the platform. eSthenos co-lending platform "CO" provides features to onboard multiple partners with "exclusive business rules" for each partner.
